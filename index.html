Account & Data Deletion

This page applies to the apps Mall Tech and Amhaz Telecom published by Dev of Beirut. It explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account (in the app)

From the Home page

  1. Open the menu ☰ (drawer).
  2. Tap Delete Account (button highlighted in red).
  3. Confirm by pressing Yes in the prompt.

From the Store page

  1. Tap the Settings icon.
  2. Tap Delete Account (button highlighted in red).
  3. Confirm by pressing Yes in the prompt.
Once confirmed, you will be signed out and your deletion request will be queued immediately.

Can't access the app?

You can request deletion by email:

  1. Email us at bob899193@gmail.com with the subject “Delete My Account – Mall Tech / Amhaz Telecom”.
  2. Include the account email or phone number used to sign in.
  3. We may ask you to complete a quick verification to protect your account.

What data is deleted?

  • Profile information (name, email address, phone number, profile photo).
  • Account identifiers (user ID / UID) and authentication tokens.
  • In‑app data (balances, purchase/recharge history, saved preferences).
  • Device/notification tokens (used for push notifications).
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What may be retained (and why)?

To meet legal, security, and anti‑fraud obligations, some records may be retained for a limited period and then deleted or irreversibly anonymized:

  • Transaction receipts/IDs and anti‑fraud logs (retained up to 90 days).
  • Server backup snapshots (purged within 30 days).
  • Customer support emails or tickets you sent to us (retained as required by law or until your request is resolved).

We do not retain your profile or sign‑in credentials after account deletion.

How long does deletion take?

  • Active records are removed immediately after confirmation.
  • Backups are purged automatically within 30 days.
  • Any legally required logs are kept for up to 90 days and then deleted or anonymized.

Partial deletion

We currently do not offer partial data deletion without deleting the account.
