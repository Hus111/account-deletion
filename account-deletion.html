Nova Telecom - Account & Data Deletion

This page applies to the Nova Telecom app published by Dev of Beirut. It explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account (in the app)

  1. Open the menu (☰)
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm by pressing Yes
Once confirmed, your account deletion request will be processed immediately.

Can't access the app?

Email us at:

dev.of.beirut@gmail.com

Subject: Delete My Account – Nova Telecom

What data is deleted?

  • Profile information
  • Account data
  • Usage data
  • Notification tokens
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How long does deletion take?

  • Immediate removal after confirmation
  • Backups removed within 30 days
